ANALYTICAL SYNTHESIS FOR DEMO (MOVE 2)
========================================================

Surface read: CEO sold $143M just before earnings = sophisticated insider going negative

Deeper read:
1. All under 10b5-1 plans — pre-scheduled, not responsive to Q2 results
2. Options expiring Dec 2025 — exercise-and-sell before expiry = tax/estate mgmt
3. Arora retains ~1.1M shares after sales = still heavily aligned with stock performance
4. Post-earnings: President selling at $203-208 (vs $187 earnings day) = stock recovered
5. No open-market BUYS anywhere in the window — zero conviction expression from insiders

The meta-lesson: Raw Form 4 data looks alarming. Context changes everything.
10b5-1 vs opportunistic is one of the most misread signals in insider analysis.
